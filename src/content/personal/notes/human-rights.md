Technological systems are not neutral; they carry the values of those who build them. If advanced robotics and AI are deployed solely to expand corporate margins, they risk widening the global inequality gap.

I believe deeply in open-source systems (like ROS/ROS2 and Linux) because they democratize knowledge, allowing a student in any part of the world to build on the same baseline. True human rights mean access to resources, access to dignity, and preventing algorithmic biases from dictating the physical security of human bodies.
